📘 Community Rules

Community Safety

GeNeX is a trusted tech community built for honest reviews, discussions and product sharing. Every user must follow these rules to keep the platform safe and useful for everyone.

Trusted community. Real reviews. Safe discussions.

Rule 1 : No Fake Product Links

Users must not post scam links, fake Amazon links, phishing websites or misleading product pages.

  • Only trusted product links are allowed.
  • Fake affiliate links are prohibited.
  • Scam promotions will result in suspension.

Rule 2 : Respect Every User

GeNeX supports healthy discussions and respectful communication.

  • No abusive language
  • No bullying or harassment
  • No hate speech
  • No personal attacks

Rule 3 : No Bad Pictures

Adult content, violent images or disturbing media are not allowed.

  • No NSFW content
  • No graphic violence
  • No harmful uploads

Ban System

Users who repeatedly break rules may receive temporary or permanent bans.

  • 1st mistake → 1 hour ban
  • 2nd mistake → 6 months ban
  • 3rd mistake → 1 year ban

Community Reporting

Users can report fake links, spam posts or harmful activity. GeNeX moderators may review and remove violating content.

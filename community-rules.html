GeNeX Community Rules

Trusted tech community for honest discussions, real reviews, verified product links and safe interactions.

Community Safety First

GeNeX is built for genuine tech discussions, honest product reviews and safe community interaction. Every member must follow the platform rules to keep the experience clean, trusted and useful for everyone.

1

No Fake Product Links

Scam websites, phishing links and fake affiliate links are strictly prohibited.

  • Only trusted shopping links allowed
  • No fake Amazon product pages
  • No scam promotions or malware links
  • Misleading tech ads are banned
2

Respect Every User

Healthy communication helps the community grow better.

  • No abusive language
  • No bullying or harassment
  • No hate speech
  • No personal attacks
3

Safe Media Only

Harmful or disturbing uploads are not allowed.

  • No NSFW content
  • No graphic violence
  • No harmful uploads
  • No illegal content
4

No Spam Activity

Spam ruins discussions and lowers trust inside the platform.

  • No repeated comments
  • No fake engagement farming
  • No unwanted promotions
  • No bot activity

Ban System

Repeated rule violations may result in temporary or permanent account suspension.

1st

1 Hour Ban

2nd

6 Months Ban

3rd

1 Year Ban

Community Reporting

Users can report fake links, spam posts, harmful content or suspicious activity. GeNeX moderators may review and remove violating content immediately.
